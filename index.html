About

I'm an undergraduate in the Department of Linguistics at UC Santa Cruz, broadly interested in formal systems of the syntax-semantics interface.

I'm currently a departmental Undergraduate Research Fellow under Pranav Anand, studying demographic bias with predicate choices in large language models. I spent the past year as a university Koret Scholar under Haoze Li on the topic of relative measure phrase semantics.

Check out my CV (see tab above) for more info, or contact me at anmkato@ucsc.edu.
