About

I'm an undergraduate in the Department of Linguistics at the University of California, Santa Cruz, largely interested in formal systems of morphosyntax and its interface with semantics.

Some ongoing topics I'm investigating include quantificational properties of measure phrases, the syntax of binding-as-agreement, and strict/sloppy VP-ellipsis with intensifiers (under Haoze Li). I'm also a departmental Undergraduate Research Fellow under Pranav Anand, studying bias in large language models.

Previously, I've also worked in the Tech4Good Lab led by David Lee, and in group research under Razvan Marinescu in Machine Learning for Molecular Dynamics (ML for MD).
